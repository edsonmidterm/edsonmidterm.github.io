Grandfather

"Foundation"

August 12, 2018

I have a cool and very kind grandfather, he told of the hardships they had with his children and his wife. He experienced all the hardships and bitter heaps that the Lord had blessed them for and did not forsake them in fighting for poverty. He had a lot of work during his adulthood he had been a tricycle driver before, he worked hard to build a business and eventually developed it. He has been my partner with my grandmother in the development of life and fulfilling their dreams, and they pursue to strengthen the whole family.
He grew up in Zamboanga so most of the home-based communication is Chavacano stories were similar to the Spanish language. I've learned a lot of lessons from him, and we often talked about his life and I had the inspiration from that aspects to continue my study and to reach my dream. I always notice him remembering my grandmother and I often see him sad. I'm happy to see the past I see my grandparents together
